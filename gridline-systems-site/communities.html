Communities

EV Charging Infrastructure for Community & Remote Sites

Gridline Systems supports the planning and delivery of EV charging infrastructure across commercial, multi-site, and community-based environments.

We focus on clear planning, coordinated delivery, and systems that perform reliably over time — particularly in locations where infrastructure, access, or power capacity may be more complex.

How We Support Communities

Site Readiness & Feasibility

  • Electrical capacity assessment
  • Site layout and placement planning
  • EV readiness evaluation

System Design & Planning

  • Level 2 and DC charging strategies
  • Load management and scalability
  • Integration with existing infrastructure

Funding Alignment

  • Electrical capacity assessment
  • Site layout and placement planning
  • EV readiness evaluation

Project Coordination

  • Utility coordination (e.g. BC Hydro)
  • Equipment and contractor alignment
  • Clear, structured delivery process

Implementation & Delivery

  • Clean, code-compliant installation
  • Consistent execution across sites
  • Minimal disruption to operations

Our Approach

We take a long-term view of infrastructure — ensuring systems are reliable, scalable, and appropriate for each community’s environment and priorities.

Our role is to simplify decisions, reduce risk, and support communities in delivering projects that work both now and in the future.
